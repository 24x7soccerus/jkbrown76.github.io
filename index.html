Soccer Juggle Journal

Welcome to my juggle journal

My name is Jack. I just turned eight years old and I love to play soccer. My favorite player is Lionel Messi. When I grow up I want to be a professional soccer player.

On April 1, 2014, I started a juggle journal to track how many juggles I can get in a row. Before I started my juggle journal my record was 203 juggles in a row. In the first three weeks I broke my record six times.

My current record is 983 juggles in a row!

Here is a video of me juggling during my first week.

How the juggle journal works

I juggle for at least 15 minutes every day and write down my highest number of juggles I get in a row and add them to this site. Right now I can also juggle a total of about 1,300 times in 15 minutes.

I love to juggle a soccer ball. It helps with my touch and gives me lots of confidence playing soccer. I hope I can help other kids to start juggle journals and track their juggles too.

Juggling for a cause

On April 21, 2014, I started working with Charity Ball to work towards getting new, quality soccer balls to kids in poverty-stricken communities around the world.

A couple of examples of donations could be donating an amount based on my highest number of juggles in a row for a specific time period or a donation of $25, which provides a new soccer ball directly to a child.
