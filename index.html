Soccer Juggle Journal

Welcome to my juggle journal

My name is Jack. I'm 7 years old and I love to play soccer. My favorite player is Lionel Messi. When I grow up I want to be a professional soccer player.

On April 1, 2014, I started a juggle journal to track how many juggles I can get in a row. Before I started my juggle journal my record was 203 juggles in a row. Here is a video of me juggling during my first week.

How the juggle journal works

I juggle for at least 15 minutes every day and write down my highest number of juggles I get in a row on a piece of paper and then I add them to this site. My dad created this site and showed me how to add things to it.

There are a couple of rules for my juggles to count:

  1. I have to start with the ball on the ground and use my feet to get it into the air.
  2. Most of the juggles need to be with my feet.

I love to juggle a soccer ball. It helps with my touch and gives me lots of confidence playing soccer. I hope I can help other kids to start juggle journals and track their juggles too.
