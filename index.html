Welcome to my juggle journal.

My name is Jack. I'm 7 years old and I love to play soccer. On April 1, 2014, I started a juggle journal to track how many juggles I can get in a row.

Here's how it works - I juggle for 15 minutes every day and write down my highest number of juggles after 15 minutes.
